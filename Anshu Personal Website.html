About Me

Hello! My name is Mr. Anshu. I am a passionate portrait artist who loves to capture the essence of people through my art. In addition to my work as an artist, I enjoy watching movies and playing the flute. Writing poems and stories is another creative outlet for me. My ultimate dream is to make a film before I die.

Projects

I have worked on numerous portrait projects, both commissioned and personal. I am always looking for new ways to challenge myself and improve my skills. Currently, I am also working on a collection of poems and short stories that I hope to publish soon.

Hobbies

When I am not working on my art, I love to watch movies, which often inspire my own creative projects. Playing the flute is a relaxing and joyful activity for me. Writing poems and stories allows me to explore different worlds and express my thoughts and emotions in unique ways.
